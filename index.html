PITCH

In “Spirits of Bloom” the user will focus on motivating themselves to feel connected to nature and will expose areas that will make them want to live freely. As the character goes on they will be able to get a friend to come along. In this game the user will be able to go on a journey to expose new areas and will find different flowers that by the end they will be able to create their own flower bouquet to give to friends and share their journey.

THEME

Spirits of Bloom allows players connect with nature, open their minds, and explore a peaceful world. It is made to ease stress and offer a sense of calm, the game uses natural colors and visuals to create a safe atmosphere. As you journey through, you’ll discover what brings you joy, peace, and curiosity whether familiar comforts or new experiences. Share your world, reflect, and others, happiness.

MY WHY

My idea for the creation of this game was to give users a game that will be connected to nature, to incorporate to their real life as well to find it aesthetically pleasinor if they like the idea of nature and how it can be put in a game. I made the game easy to enjoy with a simple task and prizes that will keep them engaged. The message I hope to share is that nature plays a part in our daily lives with mental health and freedom. Players should feel free and inspired to remember that there are many wonderful things out there and that it is important to enjoy time with the world around us!!
